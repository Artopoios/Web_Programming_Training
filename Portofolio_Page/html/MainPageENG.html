About me

I come from the greek island of Naxos and I have lived most of my life there. When I graduated from school, I travelled to Athens to study at the University of West Attica in the department of Science and Computers Engineering. There, I enchanced greatly my skills at computers and electronics, aquiring a greater interest in programming and robotics. 3D Printing and Drone development are two of the key interests of mine.

My aim is to leave every place that I visit better than I found it. Teamwork, diligence, creativity, and the ability to share knowledge are, I believe, my strongest qualities.

What I do

During my time at the university I have participated in a couple of research teams. I have worked as part of a team or even as a project manager and I have managed to publish two Academic Papers related to Drones. My Hobbies are 3D printing, 3D modelling and lately I exersise the art of marble sculpting when I am in Naxos. I have experience working on a car rental company, by delivering, recieving cars to and from customers and managing reservations. I also believe I am self-taught. Two of my latest interests are to aquire knowledge as a Full-stack Web Developer and learn the french language. Lastly, during my time in the university, I have teached collegues in advanced mathematics to help them understand and pass successfully their subjects.
